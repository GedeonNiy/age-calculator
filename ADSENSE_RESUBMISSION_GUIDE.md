## üîç GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE SETUP (IMPORTANT)

### Step 2: Verify Site in Google Search Console

1. **Go to:** https://search.google.com/search-console

2. **Add Property:**
   - Click "Add Property"
   - Enter: `https://smartagetools.com`
   - Choose verification method (HTML file, meta tag, or DNS)

3. **Submit Sitemap:**
   - Once verified, go to "Sitemaps" in left menu
   - Enter: `https://smartagetools.com/sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"
   - Wait for Google to process (may take a few hours)

4. **Request Indexing (Optional but Recommended):**
   - Go to "URL Inspection" tool
   - Enter each important page URL:
     - https://smartagetools.com/
     - https://smartagetools.com/tools
     - https://smartagetools.com/about
     - Each of the 10 tool pages
   - Click "Request Indexing" for each
   - This helps Google discover your content faster

5. **Check Coverage:**
   - Go to "Coverage" report
   - Ensure no critical errors
   - Fix any "Excluded" pages if they shouldn't be excluded

**Why This Matters:** Google Search Console shows Google that your site is active, properly configured, and ready for indexing. AdSense reviewers may check your Search Console data.
